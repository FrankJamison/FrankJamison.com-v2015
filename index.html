BIO

ABOUT ME

My name is Frank Jamison, and I am a 46 year old freelance computer enthusiast. I have recently graduated magna cum laude from the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at National University in La Jolla, California. There I studied digital logic, computer programming, and computer architecture. I hold five associates degrees from Mount San Jacinto and Riverside Community Colleges in General Studies, Math/Science, Humanities, Liberal Arts Studies, and Social/Behavioral Science.

I am a United States military veteran, having served on active duty in both the Army and the Air Force. My specialties included multi-channel radio communications and electronic warefare systems repair.

HOBBIES

INTERESTS

I particularly enjoy learning new things both in and out of the classroom. Over the years I have taught myself how to build desktop computers from raw components, how to build websites, and some basic graphic design.

I am well-versed in the art of poetry and enjoy both reading and writing it. An anthology of my works can be found in the Amazon Kindle library under the title "A Lifetime of Poetry."

When I'm not off learning new things, I like to spend my downtime watching movies and spending quality time with my family. Ghost stories are my guilty pleasure, and I do enjoy a good action movie now and then.

On the rare occassion I have money to spend, I enjoy adding new items to my stamp collection. My father started me on philately when I was very young and looking through my collection brings back fond memories of him.
